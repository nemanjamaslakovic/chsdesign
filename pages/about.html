WHO WE ARE

CHS specializes in high end residential projects and provides exceptional design service to our clients. Our mission is to design homes and offices which represent our clients design aesthetic and lifestyle. We focus on sourcing quality furnishings, improving functionality and increasing property value. In addition to working with buyers across an array of price points, we offer a versatile aesthetic in both design and staging, effectively executing modern, mid-century and transitional designs. We are able to achieve our goals thanks to an inventory of over twenty thousand pieces, and a network of contractors and designers that bring ingenuity and creativity to every project.

On the decorating side, we have executed a full design installation, custom window treatments and custom built-in closets in as little as three and a half weeks.

HOW IT STARTED

CHS was formed in 2020 by Marko Marković. Marko studied at the Faculty of Applied Arts in Belgrade and gained his experience working for many companies for 5 years.

Each home we design is created through a dynamic collaboration between our client’s style and our design expertise. As an established designer referred by the finest brands, we provide exceptional service to our clients.
